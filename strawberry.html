Strawberry Cake

You will fall in love with this is 1/2 Kg fresh Strawberry Cake made with a homemade strawberry reduction. It's moist, tender, and the most beautiful shade of pink! This recipe combines layers of strawberry flavored cake with strawberry flavored cream cheese frosting, so you get fresh strawberry flavors with every bite.This recipe uses a lot of(Egg) whites to keep the cake light and fluffy. Filled and coated in a wonderful strawberry cream cheese frosting, this cake has strawberry flavoring through and through.
